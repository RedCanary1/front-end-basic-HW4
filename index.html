POST HUMAN: SURVIVAL HORROR - Bring Me The Horizon

Release date
October 30, 2020
Vibe
Energetic, motivational, experimental.
Favorite track
Parasite Eve
Description
So far the most experimental album by the British metal band Bring Me The Horizon. Mixing many different genres of music, from old school Nu Metal to EDM, DnB, and more. This album also has by far the most guest appearances, featuring artists such as Babymetal, Yungblud, Nova Twins, and Amy Lee of Evanescence.
Fun trivia
Oliver Sykes (Frontman/Lead-vocals) has stated on many occasions that the band often draws inspiration from a number of sources including video games. Mick Gordon famous for his composing/production of "DooM 2016" took a part in the production of the album. The song "Ludens" was made after playing Hideo Kojima's "Death Stranding" The music video also features scenes from the game. "Parasite Eve's" name is a nod to a 1998 Square Enix game and has been quoted to be inspired by "The Last of Us" and "Resident Evil" franchises. Also one of the lines "If the suspense doesn't kill you, Something else will" was originally used in the 1996 Resident Evil commercial.
